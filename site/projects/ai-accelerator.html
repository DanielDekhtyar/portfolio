Project Case Study

Technion AI Accelerator Portfolio

A curated showcase of the applied machine learning solutions I engineered as part of Technion's AI Accelerator program—each targeting real-world business problems.

September 2024 – April 2025 Role: Lead ML Engineer (Technion Cohort) Institution: Technion – Israel Institute of Technology Stack: Python, TensorFlow, Pandas, Airflow

Overview

The portfolio aggregates seven production-ready experiments: demand forecasting, anomaly detection, LSTM-based stock prediction, NLP classification, and computer vision transfer-learning workflows optimized for low-latency inference.

Highlights

  • Built reproducible ML pipelines with MLflow tracking and automated hyper-parameter sweeps powered by Weights & Biases.
  • Containerized training and inference environments, enabling frictionless deployment to AWS Fargate.
  • Delivered stakeholder dashboards summarizing performance metrics, assumptions, and rollout status.

Impact

Accelerated experimentation velocity by 3x, reduced model deployment friction, and provided a living knowledge base for junior engineers joining the accelerator.

Tech Foundations

TensorFlow scikit-learn MLflow Weights & Biases Docker Airflow
